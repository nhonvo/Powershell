# Domain 1: Responsible AI and Ethical Use

This domain covers principles of Responsible AI, bias, safety, IP safety, and the limitations of Generative AI.

## 🎯 Exam Objectives
- Understand Microsoft & GitHub's Responsible AI principles.
- Understand Intellectual Property (IP) safety, copyright, and how the public matching filter protects organizations.
- Know the limitations of LLMs (hallucinations, non-determinism) and user responsibility.
- Understand security filters (vulnerability blocking).

---

## 📖 Key Study Topics

### 1. The Public Code Matching Filter
- Scans completions against public code on GitHub.
- If a completion matches ~150 characters of public code, it is blocked.
- Admin setting cascades down from Enterprise -> Org.
- See comparison of configurations in **[[certifications/microsoft/GH-300-Copilot/Copilot Comparison Cheat Sheet|Copilot Comparison Cheat Sheet]]**.

### 2. Microsoft's Responsible AI Principles
- **Fairness**: Ensure AI systems treat all people fairly.
- **Reliability & Safety**: AI systems should perform reliably and safely.
- **Privacy & Security**: AI systems should respect privacy and be secure.
- **Inclusiveness**: Empower everyone and engage people.
- **Transparency**: AI systems should be understandable.
- **Accountability**: People should be accountable for AI systems.

### 3. Vulnerability Filter
- Prevents Copilot from suggesting known insecure patterns (SQL injection, hardcoded secrets, weak crypto).
- Real-time LLM post-processing.

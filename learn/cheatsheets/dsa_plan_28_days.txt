- Existing solutions (not committed): `dsa/Udacity-Data-Structures-and-Algorithms/`

## Goal

Be consistent and fast: recognize patterns, write correct code on first attempt, and explain complexity clearly.

## Targets (by Day 28)

- [ ] 45 problems total (minimum)
- [ ] 20 mediums (minimum)
- [ ] 12 timed attempts
- [ ] 20 reviewed with `problem_template.md`
- [ ] 1 “pattern sheet” you can skim before interviews

## Standard daily routine (60–100 min)

1. Warm-up (10 min): review 1 old mistake
2. Solve (35–50 min): 1 medium OR 2 easy
3. Review (15–30 min): fill `problem_template.md` + update `patterns.md`

Log everything in:
- `daily/tracking.csv`

# Japanese Review Plan (N5 → N4) — 28 Days

Resources already imported:
- Flashcards: `languages/07_Japanese_Flashcards/`
- JLPT exams: `languages/08_Japanese_Exams/`
- Roadmap: `languages/09_Japanese_Roadmap/`

## Goal

Rebuild N5 fundamentals quickly and push N4 grammar/vocab/listening so you can answer interview small talk and daily-work conversations confidently.

## Targets (by Day 28)

- [ ] Flashcards: 20–40 min/day (minimum 20 days)
- [ ] Grammar: finish **N5 recap** + **N4 core grammar** notes
- [ ] Listening: 12 sessions (20–30 min)
- [ ] Speaking: 16 short speaking sessions (10–15 min), recorded 8 times
- [ ] Mock JLPT: 2 mini-tests from `08_Japanese_Exams`

## Standard daily routine (45–75 min)

1. Flashcards (20–40 min) — N5 first 7 days, then N4
2. Grammar (15–25 min) — write 3 example sentences
3. Speaking (10–15 min) — shadow + short answers (record if possible)

Output each day:
- Update `07_LANGUAGE_EN_JP/jp/daily_speaking.md` (1–3 bullets)
- Add 3–8 terms to `07_LANGUAGE_EN_JP/glossary.md` (optional but recommended)

## Week 1 — N5 reset (Days 1–7)

Focus: particles, verb forms, basic sentence patterns, survival vocab.

### Day 1
- Flashcards: N5 set (20–40 min)
- Grammar: N5 roadmap section 1 (from `09_Japanese_Roadmap`)
- Speaking prompt: 自己紹介（30秒） + 好きなこと（アニメ/勉強）
- Output: write 10 lines in `07_LANGUAGE_EN_JP/jp/daily_speaking.md`

### Day 2
- Flashcards: N5
- Grammar: です/ます, ありません, 形容詞(い/な)
- Speaking: “昨日/今日/明日” で5文

### Day 3
- Flashcards: N5
- Grammar: particles (は/が/を/に/で/へ)
- Speaking: “仕事で使うツール” を説明（5文）

### Day 4
- Flashcards: N5
- Grammar: て形 + ください/います/いません
- Speaking: request + permission (ください/てもいいですか)

### Day 5
- Flashcards: N5
- Grammar: past tense, 〜ました/〜ませんでした
- Listening: 20 min (pick 1 short N5 clip/test)

### Day 6
- Flashcards: N5
- Grammar: 〜たい, 〜ましょう, 〜ませんか
- Speaking: plans for weekend (7 sentences)

### Day 7 (Review + mini test)
- Review: flashcards hardest 30 words
- Grammar review: rewrite 10 sentences
- Mini test: 15–20 min (from `08_Japanese_Exams`)

## Week 2 — N4 grammar core (Days 8–14)

### Day 8

### Day 9

### Day 10

### Day 11

- Write: “ミス→対応→学び”

### Day 12

### Day 13

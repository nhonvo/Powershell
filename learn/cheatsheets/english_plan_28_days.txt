# English Improvement Plan — 28 Days (Interview + Work English)

Resource imported:
- `languages/english/`

## Goal

Speak clearly and confidently for behavioral + technical interviews: structured answers, fewer fillers, better pacing.

## Targets (by Day 28)

- [ ] 20 speaking sessions (10–20 min)
- [ ] 8 recordings + self-review notes
- [ ] 12 “short answers” written (2–4 sentences each)
- [ ] 8 STAR stories rehearsed in English
- [ ] 4 mock interviews (use `interviewNote/interviewNote/08_MOCKS/mock_script.md`)

## Standard daily routine (30–60 min)

1. Warm-up (3 min): read 1 paragraph out loud
2. Speak (10–20 min): answer prompts (record 2–3x/week)
3. Improve (10–20 min): rewrite 3 sentences to be shorter/clearer

Where to log:
- Daily speaking notes: `languages/english/daily_speaking.md`
- Interview phrases: `languages/english/interview_phrases.md`

## Week 1 — Clarity + structure

### Day 1
- Record: read your `interviewNote/tell_me_about_yourself.md` (EN) twice
- Write: 5 sentence starters you will reuse:
  - “To clarify the requirement…”
  - “The tradeoff is…”
  - “My approach would be…”
  - “One risk is…, and the mitigation is…”
  - “The root cause was…”

### Day 2
- Behavioral: answer 2 questions using STAR (2 minutes each)
- Rewrite each answer into a 6–8 bullet outline

### Day 3
- Technical: explain Redis caching (60–90s) + idempotency (60–90s)
- Remove filler words: “actually”, “basically”, “like”

### Day 4
- Explain your Quoting API project (90s) with 1 metric
- Add 5 terms to `glossary.md` (EN ⇄ JP if helpful)

### Day 5
- Rapid fire: 10 short answers (2–3 sentences):
  - retries/backoff, DLQ, ECS scaling, DB indexing, integration testing

### Day 6 (Weekend)
- Mock interview #1 (20–30 min)
- Fill `interviewNote/interviewNote/08_MOCKS/feedback.md` (English section)

### Day 7 (Weekend)
- Review recordings: note 3 improvements (pacing, clarity, structure)
- Re-record self intro
